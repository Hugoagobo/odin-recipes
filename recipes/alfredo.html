Rotini Alfredo Pasta

A yummy Alfredo dish that requires liitle to no effort! This recipe can be done in as little as 15 minutes including clean up time.

This smooth and creamy pasta dish can quickly become a fan favorite of any household with it's citrus notes and italian flavors. Though quite heavy, you'll feel like you can eat many bowls of this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Water
  • 4 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 16 oz of Rotini Pasta
  • 1 Block of Freshly Shredded Parmigiano Reggiano Cheese
  • 3 Tablespoons of Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons of Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons of Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Lemon

Steps

  1. Begin boiling your water in a large pot on the stove. Add 1 tablespoon of your salt once boiling
  2. Bring a pan up to medium heat and add your butter
  3. Once your butter begins to bubble, add your garlic and fry until fragrant
  4. Set heat to low and pour in your heavy whipping cream
  5. Drop your pasta in your boiling water. Stir well to prevent sticking. Cook to preferred doneness
  6. Add your seasonings to your heavy cream and cook until slightly thickened
  7. Pour in your cheese small handfuls at a time until you've reached your desired flavor/thickness
  8. Add the juice from your lemon to the sauce
  9. Drain your pasta and transfer into your sauce, coat well
  10. Finish with more cheese and fresh cracked pepper
  11. Serve and Enjoy!

Rotini Alfredo Pasta

A yummy Alfredo dish that requires liitle to no effort! This recipe can be done in as little as 15 minutes including clean up time.

This smooth and creamy pasta dish can quickly become a fan favorite of any household with it's citrus notes and italian flavors. Though quite heavy, you'll feel like you can eat many bowls of this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Water
  • 4 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 16 oz of Rotini Pasta
  • 1 Block of Freshly Shredded Parmigiano Reggiano Cheese
  • 3 Tablespoons of Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons of Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons of Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Lemon
